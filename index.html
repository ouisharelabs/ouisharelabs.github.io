OuiShare Labs Camp 2014

What? Collaborative environment for hacking and visioning
Who? Coders and visionaries working on open source tools to fuel the collaborative economy
When? May 1-4, 2014, just before the OuiShare Fest 2014
Where? Paris, venue to be determined

Come bring your team/project and work on your stuff within the collaborative environment!

IF

  • you’re a coder/visionary/designer/… and want to work on creating meaningful things for common good; OR
  • you have created an open source tool and want to present it; OR
  • you want to find out what’s happening in the open source technologies; OR
  • you have an idea/project and want to create a prototype; OR
  • you’re looking for likeminded people/projects to create something bigger with joined forces, ...then OuiShare Labs Camp 2014 is for you!
